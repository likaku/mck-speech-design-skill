# Speech Style Reference Guide — 演讲风格参考

> **Usage**: During Stage 1 Briefing, present the category menu to the user and ask them to pick **one** style reference. The chosen style's key phrases, sentence patterns, and speaking habits should be woven into the Stage 3 draft — not as copy-paste, but as **flavor and rhythm inspiration**.

## How to Use This Guide

1. **Present the category menu** (see below) and let the user pick a category, then a specific style.
2. **Read the selected style's profile** — note the speaking tempo, humor type, narrative structure, signature phrases, and sentence templates.
3. **During Stage 3 Drafting**, apply the style as a **seasoning layer** on top of the speech content:
   - Borrow 3-5 signature phrases or sentence patterns (adapt, don't copy verbatim)
   - Match the emotional arc (e.g., Musk = flat → excited at vision; Jobs = controlled → reveal)
   - Adopt the humor style (e.g., self-deprecating, cold humor, roast-style)
   - Mirror the vocabulary level (e.g., Trump = simple; Ren Zhengfei = literary/military)
4. **Important**: The style reference is a **flavor guide**, not a template. The speech must still serve the speaker's actual persona, audience, and objectives. If the user's real identity conflicts with the chosen style (e.g., a junior engineer picking "Alibaba P11-P12"), adapt the intensity accordingly.

#### 1. Elon Musk（埃隆·马斯克）

**演讲风格**：第一性原理 × 极客幽默 × 愿景叙事

| 维度 | 特点 |
|------|------|
| 思维模式 | 第一性原理推导，从物理学基本定律出发 |
| 语速 | 偏慢，经常停顿思考，有时会结巴 |
| 幽默感 | 冷幽默、meme式幽默，经常自嘲 |
| 叙事结构 | 问题 → 现有方案的荒谬 → 第一性原理重新推导 → 解决方案 |
| 情绪 | 平时偏平淡，讲到愿景时会兴奋 |

**关键用语 & 高频词**：

- "First principles thinking — you boil things down to the most fundamental truths."
- "This is the most exciting thing I've ever seen."
- "The probability of success is not that great... but I think it's worth trying."
- "If something is important enough, you should try, even if the probable outcome is failure."
- "I think this is gonna be epic."
- "Technically, technically speaking..."
- "It's insane. It's absolutely insane."
- "This is really hard. Like, really, really hard."
- "The future is going to be great."
- "We need to be a multi-planetary species."
- "The pace of innovation is... is incredible."
- "Basically..."（超高频口头禅）
- "Yeah, exactly."
- "That would be cool."
- "Um..."（标志性停顿）
- "It blows my mind."
- "I think there's a >50% chance that..."
- "Let me put it this way..."
- "What people don't realize is..."
- "The fundamental issue is..."
- "We should be doing this, like, yesterday."

**典型句式模板**：

> "The reason [X] doesn't work is because people think about it as [传统思路]. But if you go back to first principles, you realize [颠覆性洞察]."

#### 2. Steve Jobs（史蒂夫·乔布斯）

**演讲风格**：极简叙事 × 戏剧悬念 × 产品信仰

| 情绪 | 克制中带着激情，点燃观众而非煽动 |

**关键用语 & 高频词**：

- "One more thing..."

- "It just works."
- "Boom."
- "Isn't that incredible?"
- "And we think you're gonna love it."
- "This is really, really great."
- "Let me show you something."
- "Stay hungry, stay foolish."
- "People don't know what they want until you show it to them."
- "It's insanely great."
- "We've reinvented the [X]."

- "Beautiful. Just beautiful."
- "The whole widget."
- "Here's to the crazy ones."
- "Think different."
- "Magical."
- "Revolutionary."
- "Extraordinary."

**典型句式模板**：

> "Today, we're introducing something that is going to change [领域] forever. [停顿] And I think when you see it... [停顿] ...you're gonna love it."
